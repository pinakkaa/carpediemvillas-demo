Carpe Diem Villas - Live Every Moment, Luxuriously

Carpe Diem Villas is a boutique community designed for those who believe life should be celebrated every day. Offering premium villas surrounded by lush landscapes, the project blends refined architecture with the comfort of spacious living. Each villa is thoughtfully crafted to ensure natural light, privacy, and a seamless indoor–outdoor lifestyle. With premium amenities, secure infrastructure, and excellent connectivity to key urban locations, Carpe Diem Villas promises a life where convenience and calm coexist beautifully. Here, every sunrise brings a new opportunity to enjoy the finer things in life — because at Carpe Diem Villas, you truly seize the day.

■ Foundation: RCC Framed structure to withstand wind & seismic loads
■ Super Structure: Conventional structure
■ External Plastering: 20mm thick Plastering
■ Internal Plastering: 12mm thick Plastering

DOORS & WINDOWS

■ Main Door Frame: BT wood Melamine polished
■ Main Door Shutter: BT and Teak Veneer Polished
■ Internal Door Frame: Granite Frame
■ Internal Door Shutter: Teak Veneer Melamine Polished
■ French Doors: Aluminum door frames with performance glass, acoustically treated/tinted float glass panelled shutters & designer hardware
■ Windows: Aluminum frames with plain/tinted float glass, Thermal sealant, provision for mosquito mesh track

FLOORING & DADO

■ Rooms: Large format tile flooring from Simpolo/Nexion/Zealtop
■ Toilets: Acid-resistant, Anti-skid ceramic tiles
■ Living Balconies: Antiskid vitrified tiles
■ Staircase: Granite flooring
■ Kitchen Dado: Glazed Ceramic Tiles up to 2' height above kitchen platform
■ Toilet Dado: Glazed ceramic tile dado up to lintel height
■ Utility Dado: Rustic ceramic tile up to 3' height

PAINTING

■ External: Textured, Smooth finish combination & two coats of exterior emulsion paint with architectural features (Asian paints/equivalent)
■ Internal: Smooth Plaster of putty finish with two coats of premium acrylic emulsion paint

SANITARY & PLUMBING

■ Sanitary Fittings: Vanity-type wash basin/counter top in master bedroom
■ EWC with vertical stack flushing system with GRAVITY
■ Single lever fixture with wall mixer cum shower
■ Provision for geysers in all bathrooms
■ Brand: Jaguar or equivalent for sanitary fittings
■ Taps: All C.P. fittings are chrome plated (Jaguar or equivalent)

KITCHEN & UTILITY

■ Water Connection: Separate municipal tap (Manjeera or HMDA water along with bore well water)
■ Exhausts: Provision for water RO system, exhaust fan & chimney
■ Utility Area: Dishwasher & washing machine provision

ELECTRICAL

■ Main: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB) for each distribution board (Legrand or equivalent)
■ Bedrooms: Power outlets for air conditioners in all bedrooms and Hall
■ Kitchen: Power plugs for cooking range, chimney, refrigerators, microwave ovens, mixer/grinders, washing machine
■ Toilets: Power outlets for geysers in all bathrooms

CONNECTIVITY

■ Telephone: Points in Master bedroom and living room
■ Intercom: Facility to all units connecting to Security
■ Cable TV: Provision in Master Bedroom & living room
■ Internet: One Internet provision in each villa

CLUBHOUSE & AMENITIES

■ Well-designed Clubhouse with crèche, Children's Play Equipment, gym, Laundry Facilities, Multi Purpose Hall
■ Swimming pool with changing rooms & open showers
■ Separate rooms for associations, maintenance, and servant toilets
■ Lifts: Passenger Hydraulic ISI certified lifts with rescue device with V3F for energy efficiency (5'x5' shaft)

WATER & UTILITIES

■ Water Supply: Centralized water supply system with Central RO system
